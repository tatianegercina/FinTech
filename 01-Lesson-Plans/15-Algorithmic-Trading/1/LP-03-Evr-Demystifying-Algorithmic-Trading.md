### 3. Everyone Do: Demystifying Algorithmic Trading (10 min)

In this activity, instructors will walk students through the mindset and steps of developing a basic trading algorithm. While the trading strategy of this particular example may not be effective at scale, the purpose of this activity is to show that the actual steps regarding the execution of a trading algorithm can be simple, and that the complexity associated with a trading algorithm more so derives from the sophistication of the trading strategy (how to handle stock data and initiate buy/sell/hold decision logic).
